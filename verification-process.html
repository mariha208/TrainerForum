What We Verify

The Verification Criteria

A comprehensive review across six dimensions of trainer quality and professionalism.

Qualifications and Certifications

Academic credentials, professional certifications (ICF, ATD, HRCI, etc.), and domain-specific qualifications verified against issuing bodies.

Professional Experience

Minimum 3 years of training, coaching, or domain experience verified through employment records, client references, and portfolio review.

Expertise Demonstration

Sample training materials, session recordings, and a live 30-minute demonstration session evaluated by our expert review panel.

Background Check

Basic identity verification and professional conduct check to ensure client safety and platform integrity.

Client References

At least 3 verified client references who confirm your professionalism, content quality, and delivery effectiveness.

Annual Re-verification

Verification is renewed annually to ensure our standards are maintained as trainer careers evolve.
