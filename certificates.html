Common Questions

Verification, explained.

How long does verification take?+

Most Verified-tier applications are reviewed within 24–72 hours. Elite and Master reviews take longer since they involve outcome and panel review.

Can clients check a certificate is real?+

Yes — every certificate includes a unique ID that resolves against our public registry, so it can be checked independently of the trainer's profile.

Does a higher tier cost more?+

No. Tiers are earned through documentation, ratings, and review — never purchased. Platform fees are separate from verification status.

What happens if a trainer's rating drops?+

Tiers are re-evaluated on a rolling basis. A sustained drop below tier thresholds triggers a re-review, not an automatic downgrade.
