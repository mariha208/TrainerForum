Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for the Professional plan? ▼
Yes! 14 days free on the Professional plan — no credit card required. After the trial, subscribe or downgrade to the free Starter plan.
How does the commission work? ▼
We charge a percentage of each completed booking — 20% on Starter and 12% on Professional. Enterprise clients negotiate custom rates. No hidden fees.
When do I receive my payout? ▼
Payouts are processed within 48 hours of session completion directly to your bank account via Razorpay. International payouts via Stripe take 7–10 business days.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel the Professional plan at any time. Your profile reverts to the free Starter plan and all data, reviews, and bookings are preserved.
Is World Trainer Forum available for corporate clients? ▼
Yes! Organisations can book multiple trainers, manage team-wide training, and get dedicated GST-compliant invoicing. Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing.
